# Additional Resources on the PyTorch Fundamentals

## General

- [Deep Learning with PyTorch: A 60 Minute Blitz](https://docs.pytorch.org/tutorials/beginner/deep_learning_60min_blitz.html). A hands-on sprint through tensors, autograd, and training a tiny neural net entirely in PyTorch

- [3Blue1Brown: Neural Networks (Ch. 1–2)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDNU6R1_67000Dx_ZCJB-3pi&utm_source=chatgpt.com). Visual, intuition-first walkthrough of neurons, weights, and gradient descent/backprop basics.

- [Goodfellow, Bengio & Courville — Deep Learning (Ch.1–6)](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/). A rigorous foundation covering core math, optimization, and the basic anatomy of deep networks.

- Christopher Bishop. _Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning_. The gold-standard mathematical treatment of probabilistic models and learning algorithms.

- Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio & Aaron Caurvila. _Deep Learning_, MIT Press, 2016. The definitive textbook on DL theory, covering optimization, regularization, CNNs, and more.

- Michael Nielsen. _Neural Networks and Deep Learning_. A gentle, interactive explanation of backpropagation and core DL ideas. http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com

- Aurelian Geron, _Hands-on Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn and Pytorch_, O’REILLY, 2025. A practical, project-driven guide to building, evaluating, and shipping ML systems.

## PyTorch Specific

- Luca Antiga, Eli Stevens, Howard Huang, Thomas Viehmann. _Deep Learning with PyTorch_, Second Edition, Manning, 2025. End-to-end patterns and recipes for building, training, and deploying PyTorch models. Manning Publications

- Jeremy Howard, Sylvain Gugger. _Deep Learning for Coders with Fastai and PyTorch: AI Applications Without a PhD_. O'Reilly, 2025. A top-down, PyTorch-first course that gets you training strong models quickly with real projects.
